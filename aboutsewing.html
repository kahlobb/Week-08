Finding the Right Machine for You

It's important when making the decision to invest in your new hobby, that you find the right machine to suit your needs.
Consider the types of projects you would like to work on.
Are you more interested in designing apparel? Are you wanting to customize garments with embroidery? Should you consider machines that provide various functions?
All are important questions to keep in mind.
Once you've narrowed down your needs, it's time to compare machines!

Below is an example of comparing machines based on needs and skill-level
